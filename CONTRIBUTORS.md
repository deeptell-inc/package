# Contributors

`organic-qc-bench` is developed and maintained by the following
people. Authorship and roles follow the
[CRediT](https://credit.niso.org/) taxonomy.

## Core authors

  Corresponding author and project lead.
  ORCID [0000-0001-8381-8323](https://orcid.org/0000-0001-8381-8323).

* **Taiki Tanimae** —
  Co-author at QIRI (Quantum Integrated Research Institute Inc.).
  *Investigation of the organic-host noise-parameter profiles used

  parameterisation that informs `photoswitch.py`, and review of the
  manuscript.*

## How to be added

If you contribute code, tests, documentation, or a substantive bug
report, please open a pull request that adds your name (with the
relevant CRediT roles) to this file. Contributions of any size are
welcome.
